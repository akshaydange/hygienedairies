About Us

Hygiene Dairies is a professionally managed group committed to provide high quality, pure, fresh, healthy and wholesome milk and milk’s products to its customers.

It is situated at Dhanore village of Niphad taluka in Nashik district. Gifted with good ventilation, ample water availability throughout the year, good sunlight, good soil fertility and good connectivity to Nashik, it presents a well organized farm with all the necessary infrastructure for maintaining animals in their best health.

We have carefully selected herd of HFcrossbreed cows and buffaloes of good breeds such as Murrah, Mahsana and Jaffrabadi maintained under close supervision of our qualified and experienced dairy professionals.

About Us

Animal housing is so designed as to provide clean surroundings, hygienic and comfortable environment that allows animals and workers freedom of movement exposure to sufficient sunlight and ventilation that keeps them healthy to give you healthy milk.

Special care is given on animal’s nutrition. Balanced ration formulation, clean drinking water and succulent home grown green fodder that keeps our animal healthy to give optimum milk production.

Regular health check up is done time to time by the qualified veterinarians and vaccination too is done on time of all the animals. Our workers are trained to maintain cleanliness and hygiene in the farm.

The Chairman

Rasheed Arakkal is the founder and chairman of Hygiene Dairies.

An Electrical engineer by profession and an agriculturist by heart interested in development of rural India, has wide national and international business exposure.

His willingness to make a contribution to the society through pure and fresh milk and milk products on one hand and development of high pedigree cows and buffaloes on the other resulted in establishment of this company.

Technical Head & General Manager

Abhay Shankar is a graduate in Dairy Technology from National Dairy Research Institute,Karnal with over 26 years experience in several dairy organisations in various positions.He has wide experience in dairy processing operations as well as commercial dairy farming.He has been involved in entrepreneurship development, training and consultancy for many years,is currently functioning as Technical Head and General Manager of the company.
